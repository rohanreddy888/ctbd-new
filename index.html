Know about Thalassemia

Thalassemia is a disorder of the blood in which a child is born with inability to form normal hemoglobin

Recognize Thalassemia

Thalassemia makes child very weak and unable to do many activities that are normal for a healthy child

How can you Help?

Awareness Programmes, by getting yourselves and our children Screened & by Donating Blood

Early Diagnosis Can Save Lives

Thalassemia screening test should be done to detect a carrier

It is estimated that there are 40 million carriers among Indians. Approximately in one of 25 Indians is a carriers of Thalassemia gene.
